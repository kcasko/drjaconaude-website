About Dr. Jaco Naude

A lifetime dedicated to understanding the human mind and spirit

A Journey of Many Paths

At 85 years young, Dr. Jaco Naude continues to learn, grow, and help others. His journey from priest to psychologist represents a life devoted to understanding and healing the human condition.

From Spiritual to Scientific

Dr. Naude's journey began in the ministry, where he served as a priest and developed a deep understanding of the human spirit. This spiritual foundation would later inform his unique approach to psychological healing.

Driven by an insatiable curiosity, he went on to study theology, chemistry, and eventually found his calling in clinical psychology. Each discipline added new dimensions to his understanding of what it means to be human.

A Lifelong Learner

Perhaps most remarkably, Dr. Naude is currently pursuing his 9th doctoral degree. His commitment to learning demonstrates that growth and discovery have no age limit.

A Gift for Languages

Dr. Naude speaks 11 languages, including Latin. This linguistic ability reflects his dedication to connecting with people from all walks of life and understanding different perspectives on the human experience.

40+ Years of Healing

Since 1989, Dr. Naude has practiced clinical psychology in Pretoria, South Africa. Through Ego State Therapy, Medical Hypnoanalysis, and Integrative Hypnotherapy, he has helped thousands of individuals find peace, understanding, and healing.
