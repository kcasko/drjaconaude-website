Frequently Asked Questions

How do virtual sessions work?

Virtual sessions are conducted via secure video call. You'll receive a link before your appointment. All you need is a private space and a stable internet connection.

What happens in the first session?

The initial consultation is an opportunity to discuss your concerns, learn about your history, and determine the best therapeutic approach for your needs.

How long does therapy take?

This varies depending on your individual needs and goals. Some issues can be addressed in a few sessions, while others benefit from longer-term work.

Is hypnotherapy safe?

Yes, when conducted by a trained professional. You remain in control throughout the session and cannot be made to do anything against your will.
